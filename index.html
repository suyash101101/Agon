Overview

Agon: The Archaic Age is a competitive engine-building game set in ancient Greece. Players travel through three sacred domains — recruiting Shades of fallen heroes, calling upon gods, and offering resources to earn Patron Points. After 6 rounds of rivalry and strategy, the player with the most Patron Points wins.

Your Turn

On your turn, take exactly 1 action. The engine fires at round start before anyone acts — your cards are already working for you.

⚫
Visit Hades' Domain — Acquire a Card
Pay the ambrosia (or nectar) cost shown on any face-up card in the market. Add it to your hand. The market immediately refills from the deck.

Market always has: 5 spirit cards + 2 god cards face-up.
⚡
Visit Zeus' Domain — Gain Resources
Gain 2 ambrosia OR 1 nectar (your choice). For each card in your engine with a Zeus symbol (⚡), gain +1 extra ambrosia on top.

Always available — no prerequisites. You can always do something.
▶
Play a Card — Build Your Engine
Move a card from your hand to your play area (engine). Trigger its On Play effect immediately. From the next round onward, it produces resources automatically at round start.

No cost to play — the cost was paid when acquiring at Hades' domain.
🔱
Visit Poseidon's Domain — Score Patron Points
Spend resources to earn PP. Base rates: 3 ambrosia → 1 PP, or 1 nectar → 2 PP. Each Poseidon card (🔱) in your engine gives +1 PP per visit.

You may spend as much as you want in one visit.

Free Actions — No Action Cost

These can be used at any point during your turn without spending your 1 action.

📜
Complete a Quest
Reveal a quest card from your hand if its condition is currently met. Gain the PP shown and place it face-up in your score pile. Each player may complete up to 2 quests per game.
✕
Emergency Discard
Discard any 1 card from your hand → gain 1 ambrosia immediately. Anti-deadlock safety valve. If you ever feel stuck, this always gets you moving.

Round Structure

6 rounds. Each round follows the same sequence.

1
Round Start — Engines Fire
All players simultaneously collect resources from their engines. Each card in your play area produces its listed resource. This happens before any player takes a turn — automatic and simultaneous.
2
Goal Reveal
If not already visible, flip the top card of the round goal deck face-up. All players can see what they are competing for this round.
3
Player Turns (Clockwise)
Starting with the first player, each player takes their 1 action. Free actions (quest completion, emergency discard) may be used at any time during your turn. Play continues clockwise until all players have gone.
4
Round End — Score the Goal
Check the current round goal card. The qualifying player(s) receive the stated Patron Points. Place the scored goal card face-up in the discard pile.
5
Pass First Player Marker
The first player marker passes clockwise. Everyone gets to go first across the 6-round game — first access to the market matters.
After Round 6 — Final Scoring: Add all Patron Points from round goals + quest cards + PP values on god cards + PP values on spirit cards. Highest total wins. Tiebreaker: most resources remaining (ambrosia + nectar), then most god cards.

Final Scoring

At the end of Round 6, add all PP from every source:

SourceAmount
PP from Round GoalsTracked on score track during play
PP from Completed Quest Cards2–5 PP each · up to 2 quests per player
PP value of God Cards in Engine2–3 PP each
PP value of Spirit Cards in Engine1–2 PP each
Shade of the Keeper bonus (if applicable)+1 PP per 4 ambrosia held

Tiebreakers (apply in order)

  • 1st: Most resources held (ambrosia + nectar combined).
  • 2nd: Most god cards in engine.
  • 3rd: Most spirit cards in engine.

Game Setup ~5 minutes

1
Place the board
Separate spirit cards and god cards into two shuffled face-down decks.
2
Set up the market
Reveal 5 spirit cards face-up in Hades' domain. Reveal 2 god cards face-up in the god section.
3
Prepare round goals
Shuffle the 6 round goal cards. Place face-down in the goal slot. Reveal the top one — this is Round 1's goal.
4
Deal quest cards
Shuffle all quest cards. Deal 3 quest cards to each player. Each player secretly keeps 2 and returns 1 face-down to the bottom of the quest deck.
5
Give starting resources
Give each player: 4 ambrosia tokens + 2 spirit cards (dealt from the deck into hand — not from the market).
6
Begin
Youngest player takes the first player marker. The great Agon begins!

Quick Reference — Player Aid

Print and cut — give one to each player.

YOUR TURN — TAKE 1 ACTION

  • ⚫ Hades: Acquire a card (pay its cost)
  • ⚡ Zeus: +2 ambrosia or +1 nectar (+ engine bonus)
  • ▶ Play: Move card from hand to engine
  • 🔱 Poseidon: Score PP (3 amb=1PP / 1 nec=2PP)

FREE — NO ACTION NEEDED

  • 📜 Reveal a quest you've met → gain quest PP
  • ✕ Discard 1 card → gain 1 ambrosia

Quest limit: 2 per player. Engine fires FREE at round start.

POSEIDON SCORING

  • 3 ambrosia → 1 PP (base)
  • 1 nectar → 2 PP (base)
  • +1 PP per 🔱 card in engine per visit

  • 1. Engines fire (free)
  • 2. Reveal round goal
  • 3. Player turns (1 action each)
  • 4. Score round goal
  • 5. Pass first player marker

After round 6 — total all PP. Tiebreaker: most resources, then most god cards.
